Top Anime Genres

Shonen

Shonen (pronounced as "shown-in") is the action style of anime made specifically for younger boys. It is made famous on television by titles such as Bleach, One Piece, and Naruto. In this type of genre, usually, there is the main character, normally a muscular man, who has good martial arts skills and can increase their strength.
