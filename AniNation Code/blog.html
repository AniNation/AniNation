What is Anime and why should you watch it?

Anime, short for animation, is hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from the island nation of Japan. Anime is different from cartoons because anime has its own distinct art style, so it is important to know that all animes are considered as cartoons, but not all cartoons are considered as anime.


Anime like any other form of entertainment is liked by different kinds of people, so it is entirely up to you if you want to give anime a chance. With that being said these are the reasons why you should watch anime. Anime is the perfect form of media when you want to spice up the shows you watch, this is because anime has a vast catalogue of genres you can pick from and each anime has their own unique art style and form of storytelling. Anime is unique from other form of medias because of the vast amount of art styles present it goes from simplistic and meant for children to dark with a bit of realism, its colors also vary from vibrant all the way to monochrome. There are also countless different shows airing everyday with options reaching from anime decades ago. Anime is accessible to any kind of person from any age group be it children or seniors there is an anime out there for you. Anime offers more complex stories with in-depth themes and character developments. When watching an anime, a story line can last multiple episodes whereas its western counterparts work in an episodic way of storytelling.
